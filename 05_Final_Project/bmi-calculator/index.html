About BMI

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a person's weight in kilograms divided by the square of height in meters. It's an inexpensive screening method for weight category.

Limitations

BMI doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, overall body composition, and racial/sex differences. It's a general indicator, not a diagnostic tool.

Medical Advice

If you have concerns about your BMI or weight, consult with a healthcare provider who can perform a comprehensive health assessment.
