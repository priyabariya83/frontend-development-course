Creamy Oreo Shake

Prep: 10 mins Serves: 1 Difficulty: Easy Calories: 420
Restaurant Price: â‚¹180

Ingredients

  • 8 Oreo cookies
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream for topping
  • Crushed Oreo for garnish
  • Cherry for decoration

Instructions

  1. Place 6 Oreo cookies in a blender.
  2. Add vanilla ice cream and cold milk.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Drizzle chocolate syrup inside the glass.
  5. Pour the shake into the glass.
  6. Top with whipped cream.
  7. Garnish with crushed Oreo and cherry.
  8. Serve immediately with straw.

Tips

  • Use very cold milk for thicker shake
  • Add extra ice cream for creamier texture
  • Chill the glass before serving
  • Use fresh Oreo cookies for best taste
